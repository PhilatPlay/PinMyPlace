Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how dropLogik LLC ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our website, application, or services ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

a. Voluntary Information

  • user-selected GPS coordinates
  • user-generated location descriptions
  • email addresses (for account or support purposes)
  • device information

b. Automatically Collected Data

  • IP address
  • device type
  • browser type
  • usage statistics
  • approximate location derived from IP
  • QR code scans and interactions

c. Information Shared by Users

If you share QR codes with others (delivery drivers, couriers, etc.), those recipients may view the coordinates you selected.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • operate and improve the Service
  • generate QR codes and map links
  • ensure platform functionality
  • prevent misuse or fraud
  • comply with legal obligations

We do not sell user information to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However:

  • no system is 100% secure
  • transmitting coordinates or sensitive information is done at your own risk

We are not liable for unauthorized access, breaches, or third-party security failures.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share limited information with:

  • mapping providers (e.g., Google Maps)
  • cloud hosting services
  • analytics tools
  • law enforcement (only if legally required)

We do not sell or trade your personal information.

5. International Users

If you access dropLogik outside the United States:

  • your data may be processed in the U.S.
  • your region's privacy laws may differ
  • you consent to this transfer by using the Service

6. User Responsibility With Shared QR Codes

If you share QR codes, you acknowledge:

  • recipients may access the pinned coordinates
  • recipients may further share the link
  • dropLogik cannot control recipient actions

7. Cookies

We may use cookies for functionality, analytics, and user experience improvements.

You may disable cookies, but some features may not function properly.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates take effect upon posting to the site.
